Suman's Streak, your personal development journey that lasts for 100 days. This challenge is designed to help you transform your life by building sustainable, healthy habits and improving your mental, physical, and emotional well-being. 100 days may sound like a lot, but it's the perfect time to create lasting change. By the end of the challenge, you'll be a better version of yourself, ready to take on the new year with confidence and energy. To be honest its not just excalty about new year, its about improving your lifestsyle, self-care, its about fixing your mental health and commiting to you and your goals.

What You’ll Do in Suman's Streak: Eye Exercises: Improve your eyesight and reduce strain by doing eye exercises 3 times a day. Hydration: Drink 3 liters of water daily to stay hydrated and improve overall health. Healthy Eating: Lower your sugar intake and fuel your body with balanced, nutritious food. Daily Exercise: Get 30 minutes of exercise every day—whether it’s yoga, running, walking, pilates, or any form of movement. Meditation: Dedicate 10 minutes each day to meditation, reducing stress and boosting mental clarity. Sleep: Commit to getting enough sleep—wake up at 6 AM and go to bed at 10 PM to ensure your body and mind are well-rested. Digital Detox: Eliminate time-wasting apps like Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat, and YouTube to reclaim your time and focus on what matters. Morning Routine: Establish a structured morning routine to kick-start each day with energy and purpose.

The 100-Day Challenge: 100 Days of Growth: Each day, you’ll build on the habits that will help you improve in multiple areas of life. It's not about perfection—it's about consistency and progress.
